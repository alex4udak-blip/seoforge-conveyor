Recognizing Problem Gambling

Understanding the warning signs is the first step toward maintaining healthy gaming habits.

Warning Signs to Watch For

If you or someone you know exhibits any of these behaviors, it may indicate a gambling problem:

  • Spending more money or time on gambling than intended
  • Gambling with money needed for essential expenses
  • Borrowing money or selling possessions to fund gambling
  • Feeling restless or irritable when trying to cut down
  • Gambling to escape problems or relieve negative emotions
  • Chasing losses by gambling more to recover money
  • Lying to family or friends about gambling activities
  • Neglecting work, school, or family responsibilities
  • Feeling guilty or anxious about gambling behavior
  • Failed attempts to control, cut back, or stop gambling

Get Help Now

If you recognize these signs, please reach out to professional support services immediately. Help is available 24/7.

Support Resources

Contact organizations like Gamblers Anonymous India or the National Problem Gambling Helpline for confidential assistance and counseling.
